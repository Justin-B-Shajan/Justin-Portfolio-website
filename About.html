About Me

Hello! I'm Justin B Shajan, a passionate Web Developer and Cybersecurity Enthusiast. I specialize in building secure, high-performance web applications with expertise in both front-end and back-end technologies.


I have hands-on experience with various tools such as Wireshark, Metasploit, and Nmap and have participated in several security assessments and vulnerability analyses. I’m constantly learning and evolving to stay ahead in the ever-changing cybersecurity landscape.


My interest in ethical hacking started early when I became fascinated by how systems work and how vulnerabilities can be exploited.


In my free time, I enjoy participating in Capture The Flag (CTF) challenges and experimenting with security tools.


I am actively looking for opportunities to apply my skills in threat intelligence, security automation, ethical hacking, and web development.
